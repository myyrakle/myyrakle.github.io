Usage

To enable $\KaTeX$ support site-wide, add this to your site's config.toml:

[extra]
katex = true

Alternatively, $\KaTeX$ support may be enabled selectively on individual pages (e.g. content/blog/my-page.md) or on an entire section of your site (e.g. content/blog/_index.md) by adding the above to your frontmatter instead. For instance:

+++
title = "My Page"
date = 2026-01-01

[extra]
katex = true
+++
